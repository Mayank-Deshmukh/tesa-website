The cultural domain encompasses the rich tapestry of human expression, including art, language, traditions, and beliefs. Explore the diverse facets of societies worldwide, delve into historical perspectives, and gain insights into the interconnectedness of cultures. From literature to rituals, this domain invites visitors to appreciate the uniqueness and universality of human creativity, fostering a deeper understanding of the world's cultural mosaic

The cultural domain serves to preserve, transmit, and evolve the shared beliefs, customs, arts, and values of a society. It fosters identity, social cohesion, and provides a framework for individuals to understand their place in the world. Culture serves as a means of creative expression and a source of innovation
